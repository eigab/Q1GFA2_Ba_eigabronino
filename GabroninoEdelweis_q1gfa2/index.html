Marahuyo, an ancient Filipino word meaning "enchantment," is considered one of the most beautiful in Philippine literature. It carries emotional depth and gravitas. The Philippine Department of Trade and Industry, in partnership with Duty Free Philippines Corporation and Budji Layug, launched Marahuyo, home to an impressive collection of Philippine fashion designer brands.
